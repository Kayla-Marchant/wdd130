Trips | Rafting Company

Death Valley

This adventure is for experienced rafters. There are big waves and many obstacles. This route allows for skilled rafters to truly hone their abilities and give some challenge. This trip is about 90 minutes long.

The In-Between

This adventure is for intermediate rafters. There are some obstacles and waves, though there is nothing super difficult. This route allows for rafters to improve their abilities while also going through some beautiful locations. This adventure is about 2 hours long.

The Scenic Route

This adventure is for beginner rafters. There are some waves, though most of the trip is meant to float with little effort. This route allows for rafters to gain rafting experience while getting an amazing tour through nature. This adventure is about 3 hours long.
