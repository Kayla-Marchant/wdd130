Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this website is to educate people about white water rafting. This site will gives tips on how to get better, ideas of where to go, and show all the fun that this activity can be.

Audience

Who are the target customers? Describe their life (or business) situation (age, financial situation, interests, and so on) What do they want? The target customers are teenagers and young aduts: 16-30 years old. These will be people who enjoy adventure and being outdoors. They will be relatively financially stable to be able to afford this activity. They want new experiences to have fun and test the limits of their comfort zones. What do they except to find or what questions will be answered by them accessing the website? On this website, they will find where to go for this activity. They will also learn tips about the activity and prices for all aspects. They will also be able to see how others are enjoyin water rafting. What are their needs that aren't being met? They don't know how much this costs or where to go to participate, so this site will answer those questions. How will my audience usually access my site (desktop, laptop, mobile device)? The audience will be able to access this website an a desktop, laptop, and moblie device.
